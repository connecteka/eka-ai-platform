# EKA-AI Enterprise Sales Playbook

## Target Segments

### 1. Fleet Operators (Priority 1)
**Examples**: Ola, Uber fleet partners, Delivery companies
**Pain Points**: 
- Vehicle downtime costs ₹5,000/hour
- No visibility into maintenance costs
- Manual tracking of service history

**Value Proposition**:
- 30% reduction in downtime
- Automated maintenance scheduling
- Real-time fleet health dashboard

---

### 2. OEM Dealerships (Priority 2)
**Examples**: Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata Motors dealers
**Pain Points**:
- Complex warranty claim process
- Customer dissatisfaction with service time
- Manual job cards prone to errors

**Value Proposition**:
- Digital job cards with photo evidence
- Automated warranty checks
- Customer app for real-time updates

---

### 3. Insurance Companies (Priority 3)
**Examples**: ICICI Lombard, Bajaj Allianz, TATA AIG
**Pain Points**:
- Fraudulent claims
- Long claim processing time
- No standardized assessment

**Value Proposition**:
- PDI checklist with photo evidence

- Direct cashless integration

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Lead Generation (Week 1)
**Activities**:
- LinkedIn outreach to Fleet Managers
- Industry conference networking
- Partner referrals
- Website demo requests

**Metrics**:
- 50 qualified leads per month
- 20% demo booking rate

---

### Stage 2: Discovery Call (Week 2)
**Agenda**:
1. Understand current workflow (15 min)
2. Identify pain points (15 min)
3. Quantify cost of inaction (10 min)
4. Demo key features (20 min)

**Discovery Questions**:
- "How do you currently track vehicle maintenance?"
- "What's your average vehicle downtime per month?"
- "How many job cards are created daily?"
- "What compliance requirements do you have?"

---

### Stage 3: Solution Design (Week 3)
**Deliverables**:
- Custom implementation plan
- ROI calculator spreadsheet
- Reference architecture
- Pricing proposal

**ROI Calculator Inputs**:
- Number of vehicles
- Average downtime cost
- Labor cost per hour
- Current software costs

---

### Stage 4: Pilot Proposal (Week 4)
**Pilot Offer**:
- 30-day free trial
- 5-location deployment
- Dedicated onboarding specialist
- Success metrics defined

**Pilot Success Criteria**:
- 100 job cards created
- 80% user adoption
- < 5 support tickets
- Positive NPS score

---

### Stage 5: Negotiation & Close (Week 5-6)
**Negotiation Points**:
- Payment terms (annual vs monthly)
- Custom feature development
- Data migration support
- Training package

**Closing Techniques**:
- Limited time discount (10% off first year)
- Reference customer calls
- Executive sponsorship
- Risk reversal (money-back guarantee)

---

## Objection Handling

### "We're happy with current software"
**Response**: "I understand. Most of our customers said the same thing. What would it take for you to consider a 30-day pilot to see if we can reduce your downtime by even 10%?"

### "Your price is too high"
**Response**: "Let's look at the ROI. If we save you just 2 hours of downtime per vehicle per month, that's ₹10,000 saved per vehicle. For a 100-vehicle fleet, that's ₹10 lakhs monthly. Our solution costs ₹50,000."

### "We need on-premise deployment"
**Response**: "We offer private cloud deployment with data residency in India. You get the security of on-premise with the scalability of cloud."

### "Our technicians aren't tech-savvy"
**Response**: "Our app is designed for technicians. It takes just 15 minutes to learn. We provide on-site training and 24/7 support."
