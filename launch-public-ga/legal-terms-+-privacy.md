# Legal (Terms + Privacy)

This is a **template**. Have counsel review before you ship.

### Terms of Service (minimum sections)

* Service description
* Eligibility
* Account security
* Acceptable use
* Payment terms (if paid)
* Data processing and retention
* Disclaimers and limitation of liability
* Termination
* Governing law
* Contact details

### Privacy Policy (minimum sections)

* What data you collect
* Why you collect it
* Where it is stored
* Who you share it with (vendors)
* Retention and deletion
* User rights
* Security measures
* Contact details

### Vendor list (start here)

Update this to match production:

* Hosting (GCP / Firebase)
* Database (Supabase / MongoDB)
* Email (Resend)
* Payments (PayU)
* WhatsApp (Twilio)
* Error tracking (Sentry)
* LLM providers (Gemini, etc.)

### Product UI requirements

* Link **Terms** and **Privacy** on signup.
* Link them in the app footer.
* Publish a support contact: `connect@eka-ai.in`.
* Include “Last updated” dates.
